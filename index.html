Section 01

Executive Recommendation

Emily,

I have probably spent more time thinking about this vacation than any trip we have ever taken. That is not because I am trying to engineer a perfect itinerary. It is because I want to give us the best possible chance of having a really special week together.

The recommendation is simple: three nights in London, three nights in Paris, and four nights in Grindelwald. The trip starts with energy, becomes more relaxed as it goes, and finishes in the Swiss Alps rather than at another airport or in another crowded city.

London
Jul 30 – Aug 2
Walkable, energetic and an easy first landing after the overnight flight.
Paris
Aug 2 – Aug 5
Beautiful neighborhood, pastries, cafés and time to wander.
Grindelwald
Aug 5 – Aug 9
Four nights to slow down, enjoy the hotel and work around mountain weather.

Comfort Without Excess

American Main Cabin Extra, first class on the longest European rail day, and no rental car. The trains take us from city center to city center.

Places That Support the Experience

London and Paris Airbnbs chosen for their neighborhoods and family space. BERGWELT as the indulgent Alpine finale. Every stay has AC, two bedrooms and a couch.

A Little Room for Us

Good coffee for me, good tea for you, and enough unplanned evening time for a local beer or bourbon on my side and a glass of wine or Cosmopolitan on yours.

Sound Sleeping Infrastructure

Every property sleeps five comfortably. Whether it survives negotiations among three daughters remains an open governance issue.

"Success is not seeing the most landmarks.
Success is coming home saying, That was even better than we hoped."

Cost Philosophy

We are not optimizing for the absolute lowest cost, and we are not purchasing luxury for its own sake. The choices are meant to improve comfort or create a meaningful experience: family apartments rather than multiple city hotel rooms, Main Cabin Extra rather than business class, first class on the longest European rail day, and a four-night Alpine resort finish.
